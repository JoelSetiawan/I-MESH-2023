I-MESH is a multidisciplinary group of psychologists, orthopedic surgeons, non-surgeon musculoskeletal clinicians, social workers and rehabilitation professionals dedicated to the advancement of mental and social health in musculoskeletal care. We collaborate to develop and implement evidence based interventions to support people with musculoskeletal illness and injury.
